About Papa Louie

The Papa Louie series is a video game series created by Flipline Studios. Most of the games in the series combine restaurant management, cooking, and strategy gameplay as users run a restaurant and fulfill orders for customers. The first installment, Papa Louie: When Pizzas Attack!, was released as a free-to-play Flash game on November 9, 2006. While many future installments were also released as free-to-play Flash games, the series was later expanded to support mobile devices and Steam. The web games in the series were initially impacted by the shutdown of Adobe Flash December 31, 2020, but can now be played on websites including Cool Math Games using Flash Player emulators such as AwayFL.
